About

CA Deepak Bholusaria is a Chartered Accountant with decades of experience in finance, audit, and accounting. Beyond practice, he is a passionate corporate trainer who bridges the gap between traditional accounting skills and modern technology tools.

His workshops are built around real client scenarios — every use case comes directly from the kind of problems CAs and finance professionals face daily. The philosophy is simple: learn by doing, with actual data, not textbook examples.

He specialises in making complex tools like Power Query, AI assistants, and advanced Excel accessible to accounting professionals who have no prior tech background — turning hesitation into confidence, one workshop at a time.
